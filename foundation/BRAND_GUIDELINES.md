# Nikonyx Brand Guidelines

---

# Purpose

This document defines the official Nikonyx visual identity.

Every product must follow these guidelines to maintain a consistent brand experience.

Consistency builds trust.

---

# Brand Personality

Nikonyx is:

• Intelligent

• Elegant

• Modern

• Premium

• Helpful

• Human

• Calm

Never look playful or childish.

Never look corporate.

Never look generic.

---

# Brand Promise

Everything built into one stylist.

From your closet to the mirror to your calendar.

---

# Color Philosophy

Colors should communicate trust, elegance, clarity, and confidence.

Avoid visual noise.

Use color with intention.

# Typography

Use clean modern sans-serif fonts.

Preferred:

• Inter

Alternative:

• SF Pro

• Roboto

• Segoe UI

Avoid decorative fonts.

---

# Icons

Icons should be:

Minimal

Rounded

Consistent stroke width

Simple

Never use cartoon icons.

---

# Buttons

Primary

Blue background

White text

Rounded corners

Soft shadow

Secondary

White background

Blue border

Blue text

Danger

Red background

White text

---

# Cards

Rounded corners

Large padding

Soft shadows

Plenty of whitespace

---

# Images

Photography should feel:

Premium

Natural

Bright

Modern

Minimal

Never use:

Low-quality photos

Heavy filters

Overly saturated colors

---

# Illustrations

Simple

Flat

Elegant

Minimal gradients

Consistent style

---

# Animation

Animations should feel:

Smooth

Natural

Fast

Elegant

Never distracting.

---

# Logo Usage

Always leave sufficient whitespace.

Never stretch the logo.

Never rotate the logo.

Never change brand colors.

Never place the logo on cluttered backgrounds.

---

# Voice

Professional

Friendly

Confident

Simple

Helpful

Never use complicated language.

---

# Accessibility

Maintain sufficient color contrast.

Use readable font sizes.

Support keyboard navigation.

Support screen readers.

---

# Final Rule

Every Nikonyx product should look like it belongs to the same family.

A user should recognize Nikonyx immediately without seeing the logo.
