# Nikonyx Design Principles

---

# Purpose

These principles define how every Nikonyx product should be designed.

They ensure consistency across the website, mobile application, dashboard, AI assistant, and future products.

Every designer, developer, and AI system must follow these rules.

---

# Principle 1 — Fashion Comes First

Fashion is always the hero.

Technology exists to support fashion—not replace it.

Every interface should help users feel more confident in what they wear.

---

# Principle 2 — Simplicity

Keep interfaces simple.

Remove unnecessary elements.

One clear action is better than many competing actions.

---

# Principle 3 — Consistency

The same components should behave the same way everywhere.

Buttons, forms, typography, colors, spacing, and layouts should feel familiar across all Nikonyx products.

---

# Principle 4 — Premium Quality

Every screen should feel elegant.

Use generous spacing.

Avoid visual clutter.

Choose quality over quantity.

---

# Principle 5 — Human-Centered Design

Design for people first.

Always ask:

> Does this make the user's life easier?

If not, redesign it.

---

# Principle 6 — Accessibility

Every product should be accessible.

Design should support:

- keyboard navigation
- readable typography
- sufficient color contrast
- responsive layouts

---

# Principle 7 — Responsive Design

Every interface should work beautifully on:

- Desktop
- Tablet
- Mobile

No feature should depend on a single screen size.

---

# Principle 8 — Visual Hierarchy

Users should immediately understand:

- what is important
- what is secondary
- what action they should take next

Use spacing, typography, and color to create hierarchy.

---

# Principle 9 — Trust

Never confuse users.

Never hide important information.

Always communicate clearly.

Trust is earned through transparency.

---

# Principle 10 — Continuous Improvement

Design is never finished.

Observe users.

Collect feedback.

Improve continuously.

Every version should be better than the last.

---

# Final Rule

Every design decision should support the Nikonyx mission:

**Helping people dress with confidence through intelligent, elegant, and human-centered experiences.**
