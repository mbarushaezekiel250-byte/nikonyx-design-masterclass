# Nikonyx Home Page

---

# Mission

The Home Page is the most important page of the Nikonyx website.

It must immediately communicate the product, build trust, inspire curiosity, and encourage visitors to begin their fashion journey.

---

# Primary Objective

Within the first 5 seconds, every visitor should understand:

- What Nikonyx is
- Who it is for
- Why it is different
- What action to take next

---

# Information Hierarchy

1. Navigation
2. Hero Section
3. AI Value Proposition
4. Core Features
5. How Nikonyx Works
6. Social Proof
7. Call to Action
8. Footer

---

# Hero Section

Must contain:

- Premium headline
- Short supporting paragraph
- Primary CTA
- Secondary CTA
- Product preview
- Fashion imagery

---

# Navigation

Include:

- Home
- Features
- AI Stylist
- Wardrobe
- Community
- Pricing
- Contact
- Login
- Get Started

---

# Core Features

Display:

- AI Outfit Suggestions
- Virtual Try-On
- Smart Wardrobe
- Calendar Planner
- Size Recommendation
- Shopping Assistant

---

# Design Principles

The homepage must feel:

- Luxury
- Modern
- Clean
- Elegant
- Fashion-first
- Human
- Premium

Avoid visual clutter.

---

# Typography

Use large readable headings.

Generous spacing.

Strong visual hierarchy.

---

# Animation

Use subtle animations only.

Animations must enhance—not distract.

---

# Success Metric

A first-time visitor should understand Nikonyx within 10 seconds and feel confident enough to create an account.
